# Industry

I am a computer scientist driven by curiosity and a focus on execution. An advocate of the [UNIX philosophy], I possess deep system understanding and a preference for the command line. My work involves designing robust software across all abstraction layers, from assembly to application-level development.

A supporter of [open-source software], I have contributed to and maintained various projects. I also founded the [ALiAS tech community] and have served as a [mentor] for aspiring software engineers.

My objective is to create solutions that meaningfully improve user experience and comfort.
