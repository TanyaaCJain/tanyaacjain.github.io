# How You Can Help
Your involvement can illuminate countless lives. 

## 💡 Contribute Your Expertise
Your skills can directly empower others. Here are specific ways to engage:

### Volunteer
Lead technical workshops or mentor newcomers and hackathon participants. Your guidance can help shape the next generation of engineers.

### Contribute Code
Collaborate on open-source projects. Your improvements can enhance tools used by a global community of developers.

### Donate
Financial support sustains critical programs. Consider contributing to:

**Local Aid**: Regular, small donations (like weekly food contributions) provide consistent support to neighbors in need.

**The Internet Archive**: Support preserves universal access to our digital history—books, music, and archived web pages.

## ♻️ Practice Responsible Citizenship
Sustainable habits ensure a better future. Beyond recycling, being an informed citizen is key.

### Recycle Correctly
Contamination ruins recyclable materials. In San Francisco, ensure items are empty, clean, and dry. Never place plastic bags, food, or liquids in the blue bin.

### Return CRV Containments
Redeem California Redemption Value (CRV) on bottles and cans at dedicated centers like Our Planet Recycling S.F.

### Dispose of Special Items Properly
Use the SF Transfer Station on Tunnel Avenue for hazardous waste, electronics, and large items.
